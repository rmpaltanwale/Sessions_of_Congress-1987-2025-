The 107th US Congress met from January 3, 2001 to January 3, 2003, in the years of 's presidency.

The appropriation of the seats to each party was based on the results of the 2000 Congressional elections (which took place at the same time as the election of George W Bush as President).

Senate composition

The total number of seats was 100.

The Senate had an overall Democratic majority (as the Independent caucused with the Democrats, bringing up their numbers to 51). Its presiding officer was Vice President Dick Cheney.

There were 50 Democrats, 49 Republicans and 1 Independent.

House of Representatives composition

The total number of seats was 435.

The House had an overall Republican majority. Its presiding officer was Speaker of the House Dennis Hastert.

There were 212 Democrats, 221 Republicans and 2 Independents.
