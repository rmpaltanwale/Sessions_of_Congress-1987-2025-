The 119th US Congress convened on January 3, 2025, and will be dissolved on January 3, 2027, in what will be the first two years of Donald Trump's second presidency.

The appropriation of the seats to each party is based on the results of the 2024 Congressional elections (which took place at the same time as the reelection of Donald Trump as President (for a non-consecutive term)).

Senate composition

The total number of seats was 100.

The Senate has an overall Republican majority. Its presiding officer is Vice President JD Vance.

There were 45 Democrats, 53 Republicans and 2 Independents.

House of Representatives composition

The total number of seats was 435.

The House had an overall Republican majority. Its presiding officer was Speaker of the House Mike Johnson.

There were 215 Democrats, 220 Republicans and 0 Independents.
